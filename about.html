Who We Are

Founded in 2010, Gekko Civils and Maintenance has evolved from a small team of passionate professionals into a leading provider of civil engineering, construction, repairs, and building maintenance services across South Africa. We specialize in residential, commercial, and industrial projects, ensuring every endeavor—from renovations and plumbing to electrical installations and carpentry—is executed with precision, safety, and sustainability in mind.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative, cost-effective, and high-quality solutions that exceed client expectations while contributing to sustainable infrastructure development in South Africa.

Our Vision

To be the premier partner in construction and maintenance, renowned for integrity, expertise, and a commitment to excellence that builds lasting communities.
